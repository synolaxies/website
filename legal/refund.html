Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 09, 2026

1. Contributions and Support

Any contributions or support payments made to Synolaxies Studio are voluntary and are generally non-refundable. These payments are used to support our open-source work and digital product development.

2. Digital Services

For custom software development or consulting services, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis before the commencement of the project. Once the project development has started, refunds may be partial or unavailable depending on the work completed.

3. Cancellation

You may cancel a service request at any time by contacting us. Cancellation fees may apply based on the terms of the specific service agreement.

4. Contact for Refund

If you believe there has been an error in your transaction, please contact us at synolaxies@gmail.com within 7 days of the payment.
